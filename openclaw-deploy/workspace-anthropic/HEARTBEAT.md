# Heartbeat — Imperium Capital

Workspace: ~/.openclaw/workspace-anthropic

## Daily Rhythm
- **Morning Review (08:00–09:00)**
  - Check active deals pipeline
  - Confirm LP communications
  - Update dashboard for Principal

- **Midday Check (12:00–13:00)**
  - Verify repayment receipts
  - Track borrower status
  - Escalate any defaults >7 days

- **Afternoon Review (16:00–17:00)**
  - Update CRM with new inquiries
  - Confirm LP reporting progress
  - Prepare briefing notes for meetings

## Weekly Rhythm
- **Friday Dashboard**
  - Produce Weekly Operations Dashboard
  - Include: new inquiries, active deals, LP positions, overdue actions
  - Review pipeline conversion rate

- **LP Relations**
  - Weekly check-in with active LPs
  - Confirm satisfaction, reinvestment intent, referrals

- **Marketing**
  - Review content calendar
  - Confirm minimum 5 posts/week
  - Track engagement and inquiries

## Monthly Rhythm
- **Finance**
  - Produce monthly P&L and portfolio health report
  - Confirm LP distributions accuracy
  - Review delinquency rate and upcoming maturities

- **Compliance**
  - Verify KYC/AML records
  - Confirm collateral registrations
  - Update regulatory monitoring notes

## Silence Protocol
- Stay quiet (HEARTBEAT_OK) when:
  - Late night (23:00–08:00) unless urgent
  - No new events since last check
  - Human is busy or unavailable
