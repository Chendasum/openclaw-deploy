# Tools — Imperium Capital

Workspace: ~/.openclaw/workspace-anthropic

## Core Tools

- **Credit Calculator**
  - LTV, DSCR, Yield checks
  - Enforces underwriting discipline: ≤65% LTV, ≥1.25x DSCR, ≥15% yield

- **Deal Pipeline Tracker**
  - CRM integration
  - Logs inquiries, borrower interviews, document packages
  - Weekly dashboard reporting for Principal

- **LP Relations Manager**
  - Onboarding scripts, investor briefs, FAQ documents
  - Monthly reporting templates
  - Escalation rules for LP complaints or redemption requests

- **Legal Checklist**
  - KYC/AML verification
  - Collateral registration protocols
  - Default enforcement timeline (Day 7, Day 14, Day 30)

- **Finance Monitor**
  - Loan register
  - Repayment tracking
  - LP distribution accuracy
  - Monthly P&L and portfolio health reports

- **Operations Dashboard**
  - Inquiry routing
  - Vendor register
  - Calendar and meeting coordination
  - Weekly operations summary

- **Marketing Engine**
  - Content calendar (education, LP cultivation, authority, origination, engagement)
  - Workshop scripts and follow-up sequences
  - Brand standards enforcement

## Non-Negotiables
- No deal bypasses underwriting.
- No LP capital committed without signed agreement.
- No disbursement without Principal approval + legal documentation.
- All borrower and LP communications documented in CRM.
- Brand standards enforced on all content.
